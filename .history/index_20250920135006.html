About me

I complete my undergraduate studies in School of Cyberspace Security, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications(BUPT). My research interests focus on the intersection of 'AI × Security × Network', exploring (i) how AI enhances network security and measurement, (ii) how efficient networks empower AI systems, and (iii) how AI itself can be made more secure and resilient.
